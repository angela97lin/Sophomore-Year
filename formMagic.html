How does this work?
Basically, if you would like to "rot" (short for rotate) your text, please choose "ROTify" and the number you wish to rotate your text by.
If you wish to reverse the letters of your text, ROT should be set to 0, and "Reverse my text!" should be chosen.
If you wish to reverse your text AND rot-ify, please choose "Reverse my text!" and choose the value you wish to rotate your text by.
